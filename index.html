About Me

Evan Simon Ross is as ESL instructor working at Be Fluent NYC in Manhattan. He specializes in pronunciation and teaching comparative grammar to adult students, especially those whose first language is Japanese. He is a member of the Dartmouth College class of 2013 and graduated Summa Cum Laude with a major in Asian and Middle Eastern Languages and Literatures and minors in Music and Linguistics.

Evan is currently studying full-stack web development at the first-ever Columbia University Coding Bootcamp. This page itself is one of many web development projects completed as part of this program, and includes many of the others.

In future, this page will be expanded to include projects related to Evan's many other diverse interests, including music, writing, and conlanging. Stay tuned!
